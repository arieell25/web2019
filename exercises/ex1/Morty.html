Morty Smith

Morty is 14 years old and is a student at Harry Herpson High School


Morty is a good kid but he is easily distressed. He is often reluctant to follow Rick's plans,and

he often ends up traumatized by the unorthodox and morally questionable methods Rick uses to 'fix' situations.
